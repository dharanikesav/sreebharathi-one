Our Mission

Envisage a nation where the potential of underprivileged has a key role in its progress.

Our Vision

Our vision is to ignite a strong passion in the students of financially weaker and socially backward sections, to attain administrative positions; and to extend every possible support by guiding them learn in a way that gives them confidence to achieve goals beyond their dreams; to see yoga become a part of everyone’s life in attaining mental balance; to see pollution free world by encouraging native cow-clan protection, and consumption of organic products; to encourage the community to participate in environmental protection and to cultivate a habit of ‘Seva’; to inspire the youth to follow the ideals of icons of the nation like Swami Vivekananda, Sister Nivedita and Abdul Kalam.
